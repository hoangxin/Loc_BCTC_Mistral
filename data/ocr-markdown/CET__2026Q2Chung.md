*The company's explanation is as follows: In the second quarter of 2026, the company recorded a net profit of VND 339.5 million, while in the same period of the previous year, it recorded a loss of VND 75.2 million. Due to the ongoing restructuring process which limited sales revenue, the company utilized cash flow, resulting in financial revenue of VND 457.7 million, compared to only VND 4.2 thousand in the same period of the previous year.*
